How to Use the Kanban Board

Add Task: Enter a task title and select a deadline (dd/mm/yyyy) in the add area and click "Add".

Move Task: Drag and drop a task card from one column to another to update its status.

Delete Task: Hover over a task card and click the "Delete" button to remove the task.

Columns Explained: "Not Start" for tasks not yet begun, "In Progress" for ongoing tasks, "Parked" for tasks on hold, and "Done" for completed tasks.

Use this board to effectively manage your workflow and track your progress.
